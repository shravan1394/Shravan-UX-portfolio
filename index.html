Selected Case Studies

1) Human Visual Reasoning in Natural Scenes

Lead Researcher · Eye tracking, psychophysics, free-viewing · Python, EyeLink 1000 Plus, Qualtrics

Designed controlled studies to examine how people extract scene semantics without explicit tasks. Led an undergraduate team to build a Winograd Images dataset (under revise & resubmit at Nature Communications). Analyses show that viewers form scene understanding during free viewing.

2) Eye-Movement Policies in Vision–Language Models

Lead Developer · Human/Model comparison · Python, RL, DeepSeek Janus

Trained VLMs to generate scan paths conditioned on language prompts, then compared with human eye-tracking data to uncover attention strategy gaps. Built quantitative metrics for complex social scene understanding, benchmarking Gemini/GPT-4 class models vs. humans.

3) Gaze‑Contingent Prosthetic Vision Simulator

Designer/Developer · Assistive tech · Python, Pulse2Percept, Eye tracking

Built a desktop simulation of prosthetic vision that updates with the user’s gaze, then ran Landolt C acuity experiments to evaluate task performance. Produced HCI-driven guidelines for future assistive interfaces.

5) HCI for Minimally Invasive Surgery (Master’s Thesis)

Human factors user study · Mixed Reality

Investigated whether MR tools can improve fine motor control for surgeons. Designed tasks, recruited participants, and analyzed performance—yielding design recommendations for future MR surgical interfaces.

6) Robotics & Computer Vision Highlights

Computer vision · NASA R5 (Valkyrie) · Automated chess-playing robot

Built perception for planetary navigation in simulation (NASA Space Robotics Challenge). Designed a board perception system for a chess-playing robotic arm and developed a real-time word recognition pipeline using CNNs.
